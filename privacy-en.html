Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

1. Introduction

Timestate ("We", "Us", or "App") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Timestate mobile application ("App").

2. Information Collected

2.1. Account Information

  • Name, surname
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company information (for business users)
  • Profile photo

2.2. App Usage Data

  • Real estate listings and photos
  • Customer information (sellers and buyers)
  • Appointment and task information
  • Contract documents
  • Location information (when adding listings)

2.3. Technical Information

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • App usage statistics
  • Error reports

3. Use of Information

Collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve app services
  • To manage user accounts
  • To provide customer support
  • To ensure app security
  • To fulfill legal obligations
  • To analyze app performance

4. Information Sharing

Your personal information is not shared with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Legal obligations
  • Security breaches
  • Business partners (service providers) - only when necessary to provide services
  • With user consent

5. Data Security

To ensure the security of your data:

  • We use encryption technologies
  • We use secure servers
  • We conduct regular security audits
  • We implement access controls

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your data will be deleted within 30 days.

7. User Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of your data
  • Deletion of your data
  • Data portability
  • Right to object

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The app may use cookies and similar technologies to improve service quality.

9. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Apple App Store: App distribution and payment processing

These services have their own privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.
